INFORMATION OF THE GAME

In the context of the familiar Warring States period, the main character of Sekiro: Shadow Die Twice is "Single-Handler" Sekiro, a ninja warrior (Shinobi) who has lost his hand on his way to find his master. This young master is targeted by the boy's bloodline capable of serving a dark conspiracy of kidnappers. With a real arm, an artificial arm and a "Mortal Blade" sword, our warrior will have to cross many territories, defeating many enemies from real-world travelers, good-looking samurai to the giant demons. The plot of the game was written by the development director Hidetaka Miyazaki, but he said he had "released" the other members to carry out the details of the story. The goal of this is to make gamers enjoy a cohesive story, but with a new style that gamers have never seen before, according to From Software's advertising and marketing director, he Yasuhiro Kitao. Gamers will still be able to discover more about the world in the game through descriptions of items, surroundings or stories told through book pages, but the content of the game will revolve around Single Player Lang and the guy's adventure.
